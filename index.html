01 — The philosophy

Kids don't just learn about ideas. They build them.

Learning should be active, creative, and fun. At GLOWE PLAY, students gain the confidence to create their own future through technology, entrepreneurship, and hands-on projects.

It's not coding camp. It's not test prep. It's a place where curious kids become builders.

02 — The framework

Five letters. One method that actually works.

GLOWE PLAY is the backbone of every program. Each letter is a real skill kids practice — and a real step from "I have an idea" to "look what I built."

Kids learn to think like innovators — brainstorming, identifying problems, imagining solutions, and turning a spark into a project.

05 — Questions

Things parents usually ask.

What ages is this for?

Programs initially focus on kids ages 8 to 14. We may expand age ranges based on founding family feedback.

Does my child need coding experience?

Not at all. GLOWE PLAY focuses on creativity, entrepreneurship, and modern AI tools that make building approachable for total beginners.

Where will programs be held?

We're partnering with schools, community spaces, and learning centers across Mobile. Specific locations will be confirmed with founding families first.

When does it start?

Pilot programs are planned to launch soon. Founding families will be the first to know dates and locations.
