01 — The philosophy

Kids don't just learn about ideas. They build them.

Learning should be active, creative, and fun. At Glowe Play, students gain the confidence to create their own future through technology, entrepreneurship, and hands-on projects.

It's not coding camp. It's not test prep. It's a place where curious kids become builders.

02 — Mission
The mission of Glowe Play is to empower young minds in Mobile to explore innovation, develop creative problem-solving skills, and build confidence through hands-on experiences with technology and entrepreneurship.

04 — The model

Glowe Play Youth Innovation Challenge.

Instead of running weekly classes, we run:

  • 1 annual contest
  • 3–4 workshops
  • 1 big Demo Day / pitch event
Kids submit ideas → develop them → present to judges.

Families can apply to enter. Local businesses can join the Youth Innovation Challenge to lead a workshop.

06 — Questions

Things parents usually ask.

What ages is this for?

Programs initially focus on kids ages 8 to 14. We may expand age ranges based on founding family feedback.

Does my child need coding experience?

Not at all. Glowe Play focuses on creativity, entrepreneurship, and modern AI tools that make building approachable for total beginners.

Where will events be held?

We're partnering with schools, community spaces, and learning centers across Mobile. Workshops and Demo Day locations will be shared with applicants and partners first.

When does the challenge start?

Timeline and key dates are TBA. Apply or partner with us to get updates as soon as they're announced.
